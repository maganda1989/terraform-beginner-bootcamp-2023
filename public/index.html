King Carl XVI Gustaf

Fun Facts

1. King Carl XVI Gustaf is the longest-reigning monarch in Swedish history.

2. He was born on April 30, 1946, at Haga Palace in Stockholm, Sweden.

3. In 1976, he married Silvia Sommerlath, a German-Brazilian commoner.

4. The royal couple has three children: Crown Princess Victoria, Prince Carl Philip, and Princess Madeleine.

5. King Carl XVI Gustaf is passionate about environmental issues and sustainable development.
