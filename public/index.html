Finnish Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs, then add the milk and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until you have a smooth batter.
  4. Stir in the melted butter.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat and add a little butter or oil to coat the bottom.
  6. Pour a ladleful of the batter into the skillet and swirl it around to spread it evenly.
  7. Cook until the edges start to brown and the pancake is set, then flip it and cook the other side until golden brown.
  8. Remove the pancake from the skillet and keep it warm while you cook the remaining pancakes.
  9. Serve the Finnish pancakes hot with your favorite toppings, such as lingonberry jam or maple syrup.

Enjoy your delicious Finnish pancakes!
